About Me

Hello, I'm Sandra

I'm a registered Speech and Language Therapist with over 12 years of experience helping children and adults overcome communication challenges. My journey into speech therapy began when I witnessed the transformative power of finding one's voice.

I hold a Master's degree in Speech and Language Therapy from City, University of London and am registered with the Health and Care Professions Council (HCPC) and a member of the Royal College of Speech and Language Therapists (RCSLT). I specialise in articulation disorders, language delays, stuttering, and voice therapy.

My approach combines evidence-based techniques with warmth and patience. I believe every person deserves to communicate confidently, and I'm honoured to be part of that journey with each of my clients.

The Process

What to Expect

Your therapy journey is unique to you. Here's how we'll work together to achieve your communication goals.

01

Initial Consultation

We'll have a friendly conversation about your concerns, goals, and history. This helps me understand your unique situation.

02

Comprehensive Assessment

Using standardized tests and observations, I'll evaluate speech, language, and communication skills to create a clear picture.

03

Personalized Plan

Together, we'll develop a customized treatment plan with clear, achievable goals tailored to your specific needs.

04

Therapy Sessions

Engaging, evidence-based sessions in a supportive environment. We'll celebrate progress and adjust as needed.

During Your Sessions

  • Warm, judgment-free environment where you feel comfortable
  • Fun, engaging activities (especially for children!)
  • Practical exercises you can practice at home
  • Regular progress updates and goal adjustments
